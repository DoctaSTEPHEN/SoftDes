# SoftDes
Design of Machine-learning based Forecasting for Water Consumption System

Urban water demand in the Philippines is increasing due to population growth, urbanization, and rising use of laundry services, which significantly contribute to household water consumption. Laundry businesses often lack proper tools to monitor and forecast water usage, leading to inefficiencies, higher costs, and environmental impact. With growing water stress and limited freshwater resources, especially in urban areas, efficient water management is becoming critical. A machine learning–based forecasting system can help predict demand, reduce waste, and support sustainable and cost-effective operations aligned with SDG 12.
